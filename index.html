Our Mission
Designed to connect top undergraduate students with industry experts, the Canadian Undergraduate Conference on Artificial Intelligence will provide a platform for companies to develop corporate brand recognition with undergraduate AI enthusiasts, facilitate learning about AI, and showcase the diverse opportunities available for students in this field.
Overview of Event
Speakers
